Password Manager manual

This is a simple yet very effective Password generator and Manager.


To create a password:
1.  Select the total number of character you want in your password.
2.  Check the options as you need.  It's recommended to leave all options checked.
3.  Click on GENERATE PASSWORD! button.
4.  On the right side you will find your secure password.

5.  In case you don't like your password, or simply you need another one, click on the GENERATE ANOTHER PASSWORD button.
